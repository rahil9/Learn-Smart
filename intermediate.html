Intermediate Level Phonics

  • Revision of Previous Year Concepts: Start the session with revision of topics taught in the previous year.
  • Writing of Letter Sounds /a/ to /z/: Practice writing each letter sound along with providing worksheets for additional practice.
  • Blend Sounds into Simple Words: Show how individual letter sounds can be combined to form simple words.
  • Two-letter and Three-letter Words: Begin with word families and teach how to do arm blending, phoneme fingers, and sound dots.
  • Reading and Writing Dictation: Simultaneously practice reading and writing through dictation exercises.
  • Sight Words: Teach frequently used sight words that do not follow standard phonetic patterns.
